About us

Keshav Sharma -- Sachin Dwivedi

room owners, serving their multiple needs.
Students use our platform to search and discover rooms, read and write customer generated reviews and view and upload photos,
book rooms, book room and make payments when they reach the destination. On the other hand,
we provide room owners with industry-specific marketing tools which enable them to engage and acquire students
to grow their business while also providing a reliable
and efficient booking service. We also operate a one-stop procurement solution,
We ourselves check the hygine and comfortability of the rooms.
We also provide room owners with transparent and flexible earning opportunities.
